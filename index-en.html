Who am I?

Passionate about e-learning, data science and artificial intelligence, I recently graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Python Application Development. My goal is to support your projects by leveraging my skills in Data and AI-oriented design and development.

With a background in analytical chemistry, I've developed methodological rigor and scientific curiosity that I've applied in the world of quantitative data. My experience in teaching mathematics has allowed me to refine my technical communication, writing and project management skills, strengthened by my journey as a solopreneur.

Involved in community initiatives promoting Free Software philosophy, I've enriched my profile with skills in innovation, development and training.

I offer services around:

  • development of Python/Django/Flask scripts and applications,
  • descriptive and predictive statistical analysis, leveraging advances in machine learning (ML) and deep learning (DL),
  • creation of educational content for teaching mathematics and STEM subjects.

My areas of focus include education, healthcare and sciences, where I aim to design effective and innovative technological solutions.

Outside of work, I'm passionate about content creation, especially AI-assisted role-playing games, as well as urban exploration, hiking and musical discoveries.

Need an engaged and versatile developer for your Data and AI projects? Contact me, I'd be happy to discuss your needs!
